FInal Project Site Plan

Site Name: Electrodima Crane Service

This name refers to the name of the company for electronic repairs and light hydraulics for industrial machinery and heavy-duty cranes.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of this website is to reach more clients with industrial machinery or heavy-duty cranes by showcasing the types of work we perform, displaying our contact channels, and also showing the clients and brands we work with.

Scenarios

  • Do you offer regular maintenance services for industrial machines and cranes?
  • Do you provide emergency electronic and hydraulic repair services?

Color Scheme

  • Primary: headings, main buttons, highlighted links.
    Color: #1976b6
  • Secondary: header/footer background, secondary buttons.
    Color: #153e5c
  • Accent: icons, details, hover effects, alerts.
    Color: #ffd600
  • Text: main text.
    Color: #222831
  • Background: general website background.
    Color: #f5f7fa

Typography

  • Montserrat — Headings (h1, h2, h3), logo, buttons
  • Roboto — Body text, menus, forms, general content
  • Fira Mono (optional) — Technical details, code snippets, highlighted technical sections
